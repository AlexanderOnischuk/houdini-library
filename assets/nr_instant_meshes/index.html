NR Instant Meshes

— Wrapper for the Instant Meshes command-line interface.

This digital asset can feed your geometry into Instant Meshes and read the result back into Houdini. The Instant Meshes executables are bundled with the asset for Windows, macOS and Linux.

When the asset is first used, it will unpack the Instant Meshes executable into your Houdini preferences directory.
